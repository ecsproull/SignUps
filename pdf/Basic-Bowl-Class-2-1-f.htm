BASIC BOWL TURNING  Lathe ll 

 

 Instructors — Bill Kram and Jack Dohallow

 

 Prerequisite: Lathe l or instructor’s assessment

 Enrollment: Limited to three students per class, with a minimum of one 

 Schedule: 3 hours — there is a new class each month (verify dates and times using online   wood club monthly calendar) 

 Student Materials to Buy & Prepare Prior to Class: None — instructors will prepare the needed   material for each student 

 

 Description: This is a class which applies the techniques of the Basic Lathe l instruction to bowl turning.  The topics to be covered are:

•         Getting started, review all basics

•         Tools -  bowl gouge, scrapers, parting tool – tool orientation and sharpening angles

•         Grain and cutting direction, tool control

•         Mounting and cutting bowl bottom

•         Cutting the top and finishing the bottom

•         Design elements

 Each participant will practice turning a square blank into a small bowl.

 

 Fee: $15 — this includes the instructor provided wood. 

 

 Tools will be provided, but student may their bring their own to use and to verify sharpening angles
